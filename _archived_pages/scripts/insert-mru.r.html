--- section: "scripts" chapter: "domain" title: "Insert - Most Recently Used list idiom" license: "none" excerpt: " Insert an item in a series, where the series is treated as an MRU list. That is, the newest item is at the head, there are no duplicates (inserting a value removes the previous instance of that value if it exists), and the series may be limited to a specific size. If a new item causes the series to grow beyond that size, the last item is removed. It is often used with menus of recently accessed files. " --- {% include_relative insert-mru.r %}
